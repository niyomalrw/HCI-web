Fluffy Pancakes

♥

Classic, thick, and fluffy homemade pancakes.

⏱ 20 mins👨‍🍳 Easy🍽 4 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides.

Greek Quinoa

♥

A refreshing, protein-packed salad topped with a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing.

⏱ 20 mins👨‍🍳 Easy🍽 4 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa and boil in 2 cups of water for 10-12 minutes until fluffy. Let cool.
  2. Chop the cucumber and cherry tomatoes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, vegetables, and feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Toss well and serve.

Spaghetti Carbonara

♥

An authentic, creamy Italian classic made with just 4 main ingredients.

⏱ 15 mins👨‍🍳 Easy🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the guanciale in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and grated cheese together.
  4. Drain pasta (save a little water), add to the pan with guanciale. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water until a creamy sauce forms. Serve immediately!

Baked Salmon

♥

Simple, tender, and flaky oven-baked salmon.

⏱ 30 mins👨‍🍳 Easy🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, garlic, and herbs. Top with lemon slices.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until salmon easily flakes with a fork.

Classic Tiramisu

♥

An elegant, no-bake Italian dessert featuring espresso-soaked ladyfingers.

⏱ 40 mins👨‍🍳 Medium🍽 8 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick. Fold in the mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  2. Quickly dip half of the ladyfingers into the espresso and arrange in a dish.
  3. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder.

Fudge Brownies

♥

Rich, chocolatey fudge brownies with a crinkly top.

⏱ 45 mins👨‍🍳 Easy🍽 9 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease an 8x8 inch baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat well.
  3. Stir in cocoa powder, a pinch of salt, and a splash of vanilla extract. Fold in flour just until combined.
  4. Pour batter into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
