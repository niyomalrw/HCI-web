Spaghetti Carbonara

♥

An authentic, creamy Italian classic made with just 4 main ingredients.

⏱ 15 mins 👨‍🍳 Easy 🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the guanciale in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and grated cheese together.
  4. Drain pasta (save a little water), add to the pan with guanciale. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water until a creamy sauce forms. Serve immediately!

Classic Caesar Salad

♥

A crisp, creamy, and savory classic that comes together in minutes.

⏱ 15 mins 👨‍🍳 Easy 🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry the romaine lettuce leaves.
  2. Chop or tear the lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the lettuce and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Sprinkle the croutons and shaved Parmesan cheese on top. Serve immediately.

♥

An elegant, no-bake Italian dessert featuring espresso-soaked ladyfingers.

⏱ 40 mins 👨‍🍳 Medium 🍽 8 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until thick. Fold in the mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  2. Quickly dip half of the ladyfingers into the espresso and arrange in a dish.
  3. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder.
